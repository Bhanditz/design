W3C · Design Lab

  • Get Involved · W3C Developers

    Design proposal for the W3C Developers website's get involved page. Those pages aim to explain to the community how to get involved with W3C, contribute to our Open Source projects, and join the discussion around Web standards. The best contributors should be able to get recognition on a "Hall of Fame" page or something alike.
